Profile

After graduating with first class honours, Gareth was lucky enough to join award winning digital design agency magneticNorth as a designer and worked on projects for Arup, Channel 4, Reuters, The BBC and The Co-operative

Fundamentally, he is interested in human behaviour, provoking emotions in unexpected ways and taking the alternative route to surprise and delight. Working under pressure provides the best results.
