Hello, my name's Gareth Hughes,
a digital creative in Manchester.

After graduating with first class honors, I was lucky enough to join award winning digital design agency magneticNorth as a designer and worked on projects for Arup, The Co-operative, Channel 4, Reuters and The BBC.

As a way of sharpening skills and bearing new ones, I champion side projects in both app publishing and directing short films.

Fundamentally, I'm interested in human behaviour, provoking emotions in unexpected ways and taking the alternative route to surprise and delight. My work has even made my mum cry once. Sorry Mum.
