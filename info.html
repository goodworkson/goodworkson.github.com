Gareth Hughes — digital creative in Manchester

After graduating with first class honors, I was lucky enough to join award winning digital design agency magneticNorth as a designer and worked on projects for Arup, The Co-operative, Channel 4, Reuters and The BBC.

As a way of sharpening skills and bearing new ones, I champion side projects in both app publishing and directing short films. I also enjoy writing up the process afterwards.

I like provoking emotions in unexpected ways and taking the alternative route where I can.
