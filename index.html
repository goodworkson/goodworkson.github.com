Hi, I’m Gareth.

I design, code, animate and direct experiences in digital and film. Currently as a Senior Art Director at AKQA in London.

I’ve led talented design teams across Europe and the US. Working with a global client roster to create brand identities, art direction for live events and films, interactive design systems and many pitches.

I've mentored in initiatives which empower young creatives such as AKQA's Future Academy, D&AD New Blood Academy and Future Lions.

I'm obsessed with learning new creative disciplines. From code, concept art, game engines and soundtracks for short films. A master of none, but an appreciation and understanding of the limitless possibilities when connecting these disciplines together.
