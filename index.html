Gloam

Gloam is a short animated film which depicts a solitary creature wandering a dark forest who encounters something unknown with only curiosity to lead the way.

Co-director, writer and composer

I wrote and co-directed Gloam with David Elwell who worked on the Animation. I also composed an original score for the short.

Originally intended as a showcase piece for David, a few hours after uploading, Gloam was featured by Vimeo and received over 100,000 views in the first month. We still don't believe it.

“A beautifully crafted short” — The Atlantic

“Better than titanic” — Youtube user

2 Sugars

“A neat idea that's very elegantly and charmingly implemented” — Stephen Fry

Designer, UX and Co-Creator

2 Sugars is a tool that makes jotting down drink requests a doddle whether it's your round in a small team or a runner in an ad agency

After creating the 2 Sugars 1.0 in 2010 shortly after graduating, I decided to give it a fresh look for 2.0 to demonstrate a smarter UX and more options to choose from.

2.0 isn't available yet, but v1.0 is still available on the app store.
