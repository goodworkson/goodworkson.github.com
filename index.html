Hi, I’m Gareth.

I design, code, animate and direct experiences in digital and film. Currently as a Senior Art Director at AKQA, Amsterdam.

I’ve led talented design teams across Europe and the US. Working with a global client roster to create brand identities, art direction for live events and films, interactive design systems and participated many pitches.

I mentor in initiatives which empower young creatives such as AKQA's Future Academy, D&AD's New Blood Academy and Future Lions.

I'm obsessed with learning new creative disciplines. From code, concept art, game engines and soundtracks for short films. A master of none, but a love for the endless possibilities when connecting these disciplines together.
