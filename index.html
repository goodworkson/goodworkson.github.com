Gloam

An animated short which follows a solitary character who encounters something unknown with only curiosity to lead the way.

Co-director, Writer and Composer

I wrote, art directed and co-directed Gloam with David Elwell. David tackled the VFX while I composed an original score for the short.

Originally intended as a showcase piece for David, a few hours after uploading, Gloam was featured by Vimeo and received over 100,000 views in the first month. We still don't believe it.

2 Sugars

“A neat idea that's very elegantly and charmingly implemented” — Stephen Fry

Designer, UX and Co-Owner

2 Sugars is a tool that makes jotting down drink requests a doddle whether it's your round in a small team or a runner in an ad agency

After creating the 2 Sugars 1.0 in 2010 shortly after graduating, I decided to give it a fresh look for 2.0 to demonstrate a smarter UX and more options to choose from.

2.0 is in the works, but v1.0 is still available on the app store.

Seen

Seen by mN is a Smart TV based app that allows the people of mN to share the inspiring videos they find to view from the comfort of your living room.

Lead design and UX

Designing for TV comes an extra set of challenges from a UX perspective. Navigating a Smart TV app has to be effortless and clear. Once the navigation was nailed down, I tried to display it as elegantly as possible. For example, the simple arrow which allows viewers to know what is currently active almost became a heroic character in itself.

While the UI was extremely stripped back we tried to include playful details such as a small animation on the arrow and a subtle colour change to the time line as it progressed.

AGLC Screens

Interface design for a set of large format information screens based in The University of Manchester's most recent building, The AGLC.

Lead Designer

After openening in late 2012, the £24 million building allows students to work and socialise at any time of the day

A suite of apps were created as part of a unique interface for students including a campus map for wayfinding, services and buildings information. A facility for locating computers and room availability in the building. Access to the story of the AGLC, live news and social media.

The outcome is simply a helpful experience that meets the expectations of students. Providing access to useful content and visually compliments the architecture.

BBC Bitesize

An interactive suite of revision activities for BBC Bitesize. Designed to specifically to help Key Stage 3 sudents engage with the ICT Curriculum.

Lead designer

In order to create a body of work which appealed to a young audience we fed plenty of fun into the illustrations which meant some requirements ended up as surreal visual metaphors.

A clear method was needed in place which ensured consistency for over two hundered illustrations which would later become part of the animations. A colour pallete was set and illustration were based on the rule of thirds to keep the layout consistent.
