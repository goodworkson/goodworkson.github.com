BIO

Gareth is a senior designer at the global ideas and innovation agency AKQA, London.

After graduating with first class honours in Graphic Design, he joined Manchester's leading interactive agency in innovation, mN. For three years Gareth designed for The Co-operative, Arup, Channel 4, Reuters and The BBC. Gareth's proudest achievement here, the design for BBC Sport mobile.

Gareth has collaborated on side projects to tell stories outside the boundaries of interactive design. In 2012 Gareth directed and scored Gloam, a short animated film which received overwhelming reception and earned a Vimeo Staff Pick award.

At the start of Gareth's design career, he released 2‐Sugars, a simple utility iPhone app which Stephen Fry praised as “a neat idea that's very elegantly and charmingly implemented”.

Now in 2014, Gareth aims to continue AKQA's record of gold standard of digital creativity and innovation.
