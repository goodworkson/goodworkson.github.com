2Sugars

Co-owner, UX & UI

Concept

2 Sugars is a tool that makes jotting down drink requests a doddle whether it's your round in a small team or a runner at an agency. 2 Sugars is a self initiated project inspired by my experiences as an intern during university.

Stephen Fry was nice enough to mention in a tweet that he found it “a neat idea that's very elegantly and charmingly implemented”. Thanks, Stephen.
