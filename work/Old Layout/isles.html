Isles

Concept & Art Direction

Future

Isles is a concept. So far a fully realised character design and three visual stills have been created. A screenplay has been written so in the future we may see this character discover more of this dark world in a short film or even game.

Concept

In a world deprived of light, a curious collector travels across islands shrouded in mist for items lost at sea while shadows of giants wander with unknown intentions. The Isles character must travel above the mist and clouds to see the mountain tops of other islands before traveling to them at the risk of not knowing what lurks there.
