Purescript

Website & Identity

About

Purescript is programming language written by my crazy intelligent friend Gary Burgess and Phil Freeman over in LA. They were nearing completion of the language and required an identity so that people can spread the news and a place for developers to pick up Purescript to use.

Programming language sites often fall victim to design that lacks character or perhaps sometimes a little too much. I hate to see something displayed in a way that doesn’t represent the hard work so I jumped at the chance to design a fully responsive site and visual identity for Purescript.

Outcome

The result is a fast and super clean responsive site containing all the current documentation for Purescript with the flexibility to add more in years to come as the Purescript community grows. Fans of the language have printed tshirts and stickers of the new logo which I’m humbled to see.
