Profile

My name is Gareth Hughes.

I’m a Senior Art Director and Interactive Designer at AKQA, London

I’ve been fortunate enough to lead talented teams across Europe and the US. Working with a global client roster to create brand identities, art direction for live events and films, interactive design systems and plenty of pitches.

I've mentored in initiatives which empower young creatives such as AKQA's Future Academy, D&AD New Blood Academy and Future Lions.

I’m happiest when learning something new. And always pursue other creative disciplines: front-end code, concept art, game engines and musical scores. A master of none, but an appreciation and understanding of the limitless possibilities when connecting these skills.

I love side-projects. Gloam is a short film I directed and scored which won a Vimeo award. And as a young graduate I created a simple iOS app which was praised by Steven Fry.
