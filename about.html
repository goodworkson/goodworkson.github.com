Profile

I’m an award-winning designer and art director. I’ve created design systems for global brands with teams in Europe and the US, with experience in leading others to create both scalable and boutique solutions.

Every year, I participate in initiatives which empower young creatives such as AKQA's Future Academy, D&AD New Blood Academy and Future Lions.

I’m happiest outside of my comfort zone and have always pursued other creative disciplines. From front-end code, concept art, game engines to music. Previously I directed and scored a short film which won a Vimeo award and as a graduate created a simple iOS app which was praised by Steven Fry. I believe an understanding in how things are made goes a long way.

Current side projects include: a silly app for everyone, a print series for modern homes and a game which will hopefully touch the hearts of many.
