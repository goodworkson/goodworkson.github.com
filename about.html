Gareth Hughes is a multi-disciplinary creative based in Manchester.

After graduating with first class honors, Gareth joined award winning digital design agency magneticNorth to work on projects for Arup, Reuters and The BBC.

As a way of sharpening his skills and bearing new ones, Gareth champions side projects in both app publishing and directing short films.
