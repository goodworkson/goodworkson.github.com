Gareth Hughes is a senior designer at AKQA, London.

After graduating with first class honours in graphic design, Gareth joined Manchester’s leading agency in interactive design and innovation, MagneticNorth. Over three years Gareth designed for The Co-operative, Arup, Channel 4, Reuters and The BBC. While here Gareth designed BBC Sport mobile which became an award winning app. Since working at AKQA Gareth has focussed on conceptual design for new business as well as leading projects for Studio Canal, GSK and Nike. Gareth also self published ‘2Sugars’, a utility app which was praised by Stephen Fry.
